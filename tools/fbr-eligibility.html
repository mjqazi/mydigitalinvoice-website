    What is FBR digital invoicing, in plain language?

    FBR (Federal Board of Revenue) is Pakistan's tax authority. Through the IRIS portal, FBR now requires every sales-tax-registered business to submit invoices electronically — with a unique IRN and QR code on each invoice — within 24 hours of issue.

    Who's required today

    All sales-tax-registered businesses covered by SRO 709 — typically manufacturers, importers, wholesalers, distributors, and tier-1 retailers. Coverage has been expanding every quarter.

    What the penalties look like

    Fixed penalties up to Rs 500,000 for corporates, Rs 250,000 for non-corporates — plus 0.1% of turnover per non-compliance incident. See your exposure →

    What changes in your workflow

    Every sales invoice goes through IRIS before it reaches the customer. You keep a 10-year audit trail, live IRN on the printed invoice, and automatic Annexure-C population.
