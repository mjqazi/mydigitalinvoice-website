How the math works

Plain arithmetic — no hidden assumptions. The savings come from three places:

1

Hours stop going to manual IRIS

Automation turns a 4–10 minute task into a 10-second one. Multiply across every invoice, every client, every month.

2

Those hours have a cost

Whether it's your senior accountant or a junior filer, their time is billable or redeployable. We use your hourly rate to convert hours → rupees.

3

Penalty risk is real — price it in

Rs 500,000 penalty × probability of a miss per year = annualised exposure. Most firms underestimate this until the first audit letter arrives.
