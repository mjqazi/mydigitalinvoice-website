The problem every Pakistani accounting firm faces

You handle FBR filings, sales tax returns, and now — digital invoicing — for dozens or hundreds of clients. Juggling separate logins per client, chasing invoices over WhatsApp, re-entering data into IRIS, and trying to keep an audit trail across businesses is a full-time job by itself.

MyDigitalInvoice's Accounting Firm Portal is built for that reality. One firm account. Every client under one roof. Role-based access for your team. Billing handled at the firm level so you don't have to invoice each client for the software.

Built for how Pakistani accounting firms actually work

Unlimited Client Companies

Add as many client businesses as your firm serves. Each company stays fully isolated — data, users, FBR credentials — but is reachable from your single firm dashboard.

Accountant Assignments

Assign specific accountants on your team to specific client companies. Track who did what, when, with full attribution on every ledger entry and FBR submission.

Ledger Verification & Notes

Review client transactions, add notes (with XSS protection), upload supporting documents, and verify entries as an accountant. Full 10-year audit trail with user attribution on every action.

Firm-Level Billing

Your firm is invoiced. You decide how to charge your clients — markup, flat fee, or included in your retainer. No per-client card on file. No passing-through subscription management.

Firm Branding

Upload your firm's logo and branding. Client companies see your firm branding on the accountant portal — reinforcing your identity as the compliance partner.

Bulk Upload for High-Volume Clients

When your wholesaler or distributor client drops an Excel with 5,000 invoices on your desk, upload it once, walk away, and let our bulk engine validate and submit to FBR IRIS — auto-resuming if anything hiccups.

How it works for your firm

  1. Register your firm Set up your firm profile once — firm name, address, tax registration, logo. That's your root account.
  2. Invite your accountants Add team members with roles (Firm Admin, Senior Accountant, Junior Accountant) so permissions match your firm's structure.
  3. Add client companies Create client company profiles, connect their FBR credentials, and assign them to specific accountants on your team.
  4. Your accountants do their work Each accountant sees only the clients they're assigned to, creates/validates invoices, reviews ledgers, handles bulk uploads, and submits to FBR — all with their name on the audit trail.
  5. You bill your clients, we bill your firm Month-end usage from all client companies rolls up to your firm-level invoice. You keep your client relationship and pricing.

Smallest clients (0–5 FBR invoices/month) are free forever. Scale up as your book grows. No per-seat charge for accountants.

Questions Pakistani accounting firms ask us

Can I migrate clients already on other FBR software?

Yes. We handle onboarding — including bringing over historical invoices, client master data, and setting up FBR credentials. Most migrations take under a week for firms under 50 clients.

Do my accountants need individual logins?

Every accountant has their own login tied to your firm. Every action they take is attributed to them in the audit ledger — critical for FBR audits and internal accountability.

Can I bill my clients more than I pay you?

Absolutely — that's the point. You set your own pricing and retainer structure. We bill your firm at transparent usage-based rates (including free for 0–5 invoice/month clients). The margin is yours.

What about data privacy between clients?

Each client company's data is isolated at the database level. An accountant can only see clients they've been explicitly assigned to. Your firm admin can see all — but every view is logged.

Is this only for big firms?

No. The firm portal is designed for everyone from solo tax consultants managing 5 clients to firms with 500+. The pricing scales with client count, so there's no "enterprise tier" trap.
