What happens in a MyDigitalInvoice demo

15 minutes, structured — so you get real answers, not a pitch.

1

Your situation, not ours

First 3 minutes: you tell us what type of business you run, what your FBR pain is right now, and what tool (or Excel sheet) you're using.

2

A live invoice end-to-end

We create a sample invoice in MyDigitalInvoice — ideally with your sample data — and submit it to IRIS live. You see the IRN, the QR code, and the Annexure-C effect.

3

Your exact questions answered

Rest of the time is your questions: pricing, migration, bulk upload, multi-user, accountant portal, data security. Nothing scripted.

4

Written recap on WhatsApp

You get a clean summary on WhatsApp afterwards — setup timeline, cost estimate, next step — so you can share it with your partner or accountant.

Demo FAQ

Is the demo really free?

Yes. No card, no commitment. We don't charge for demos. If you decide not to move forward after, we genuinely don't chase.

How technical do I need to be?

Not at all. We adjust. Many of our best customers are shopkeepers who never touched a SaaS product before MyDigitalInvoice.

Can my accountant / partner / tech person join?

Please invite them. We actively prefer it — decisions go faster when everyone's in the room.

Do you also do on-site demos in Karachi / Lahore / Islamabad?

For larger accounts, yes — we'll drive to you. Mention it on WhatsApp and we'll coordinate.

I already have an FBR integrator. Is it worth talking?

Often yes. Two common patterns: (a) your current integrator charges 10x more than necessary for what they do, or (b) they missed a sale-type scenario you actually use. Worth a 15-min check.
