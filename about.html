About Us

FBR-Integrated Invoicing for Pakistani Businesses

We're on a mission to make FBR compliance simple and affordable for every sales tax-registered business in Pakistan.

Our Story

My Digital Invoice was created in response to FBR's mandatory digital invoicing requirements for sales tax-registered businesses. We saw that existing solutions were charging exorbitant fees (Rs 50,000-200,000/month) for FBR integration - putting compliance out of reach for small and medium businesses.

We partnered with licensed FBR integrators to build a solution that auto-submits invoices to IRIS, generates unique IRN numbers and QR codes, and keeps Pakistani businesses compliant - all at an affordable Rs 5,000/month. Our system handles everything: HS codes, buyer/seller details, tax calculations, and real-time submission.

Our Mission

To ensure every sales tax-registered business in Pakistan can meet FBR's digital invoicing requirements without breaking the bank. As of December 31, 2025, FBR mandates digital invoicing, we're committed to helping manufacturers, importers, wholesalers, distributors, and all registered taxpayers become compliant quickly and affordably.
