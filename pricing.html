How billing works

At the end of each month, we count the FBR invoices you submitted. You pay for the bracket you fall into — nothing upfront, no subscription, no commitment. Submit 0 invoices? Pay nothing. Submit 150? You're in the Standard bracket — Rs 2,000 + tax for that month. Simple.
