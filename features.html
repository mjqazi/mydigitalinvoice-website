Features

Complete FBR Compliance Made Simple

Everything you need to stay compliant with FBR's mandatory digital invoicing requirements. Integrated directly with IRIS for seamless tax reporting.

Features

Features

Auto FBR Submission

Invoices are automatically submitted to FBR's IRIS system in real-time. Get unique IRN (Invoice Reference Number) and verifiable QR codes instantly.

HS Code Management

Built-in HS code lookup and management. Ensure every invoice has correct item descriptions and tax classifications as required by FBR.

Equipment & Inventory

Maintain a comprehensive catalog of your products and equipment. Track every sale and purchase with complete audit trails.

Sales Tax Calculator

Automatic GST/Sales tax calculations based on FBR rates. Handle different tax scenarios including exempt, zero-rated, and standard supplies.

Buyer & Seller Registry

Maintain complete records of buyers and sellers with NTN/CNIC verification. Auto-populate invoice details for registered parties.

Compliance Reports

Generate FBR-ready reports for tax filing. Track input tax credits, output tax, and net payable with detailed breakdowns.
