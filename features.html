Features

The Complete Digital Invoicing Platform for Pakistan

Beyond basic FBR compliance. Full e-invoicing & electronic invoice generation, bulk upload of thousands of invoices, an accountant firm portal, 10-year audit ledger, inventory & batch tracking — built around FBR's IRIS framework and all 28 sale-type scenarios.

Core Capabilities

What sets MyDigitalInvoice apart

All 28 FBR Sale-Type Scenarios

Standard (SN001), reduced (SN005), exempt (SN006), zero-rated (SN007), 3rd Schedule (SN008), steel (SN003–004), CNG (SN023), cement (SN021), electric vehicles (SN020), POS retail (SN026–028) — full coverage of every FBR scenario, with automatic rate resolution and SRO selection.

Bulk Upload with Crash Recovery

Drop in an Excel file with 9,000+ invoices. We validate, submit to FBR IRIS sequentially, track progress, and auto-resume from the last checkpoint if anything fails — no duplicates, no lost work. Add-on module (Rs 5,000 + tax/month) unlocks unlimited bulk invoices.

Accounting Firm Portal

Manage multiple client companies from one firm-level dashboard. Assign clients to specific accountants, review ledgers, verify transactions, upload supporting documents, and handle firm-level billing. Built for Pakistani tax consultants and accounting firms.

10-Year Transaction Audit Ledger

Every invoice, edit, and submission is logged with user attribution, timestamps, notes, and document attachments. When FBR or your auditor asks, you have a clean, tamper-evident record that exceeds statutory retention requirements.

Inventory, Warehouse & Batch Tracking

Full product catalog with HS codes and SKUs, stock movement tracking, batch receipt management with expiry dates, low-stock alerts, and stock adjustment previews. Purchase invoices automatically increase stock; sales invoices decrement it.

FBR Sales Ledger Import (Purchases)

Upload the Excel file FBR gives you (SalesLedger.xls) and we parse it, detect new/changed/unchanged invoices, auto-create suppliers, link purchases to your warehouse items by HS code, and keep a 10-year audit trail of every purchase.

More Features

Every detail you need for FBR & beyond

Real-Time IRN & FBR QR Codes

Every validated invoice receives an Invoice Reference Number and an FBR-verifiable QR code from IRIS within seconds — print, email, or share on WhatsApp.

Live FBR Rate Lookup & Caching

Further tax rates pulled live from FBR (by buyer registration type), cached for performance, so bulk runs don't stall. Rates refresh automatically every few hours.

Client Registry with FBR Verification

Add customers by NTN or CNIC — we verify registration status against the FBR registry, auto-populate addresses and province, and flag mismatches before they reach IRIS.

WhatsApp, Email & PDF Sharing

Share invoices over WhatsApp (direct link), email via Resend, or download a branded PDF. Every customer-facing document carries your logo, banking details, and registration text.

Role-Based Multi-User Access

Owner, Admin, User, Accountant, and Sales Rep roles with granular permissions. Invite team members, track every action, and restrict who can submit to FBR.

POS / Retail Invoicing Mode

Dedicated point-of-sale flow for retailers handling FBR scenarios SN026–SN028 (standard, 3rd Schedule, reduced rate retail). Fast checkout UI with customer-facing print.

Shopify Integration (Add-On)

Auto-generate FBR invoices from Shopify orders. From Rs 1,000 + tax/month on paid brackets; free bracket users pay nothing extra.

Compliance Dashboard & Analytics

See FBR submission status, sales-tax due, invoice volume by scenario, and compliance health at a glance. Exports for filing and internal reporting.

Live CNIC & NTN Verification

Verify buyer registration type in real time against FBR's Get_Reg_Type API. Prevents submission failures caused by wrong registration-type assumptions.
