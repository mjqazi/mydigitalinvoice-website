2. Description of Service

My Digital Invoice is a cloud-based FBR-integrated digital invoicing platform that allows users to create, submit, and manage FBR-compliant invoices. The Service includes features such as:

  • FBR IRIS integration for automatic invoice submission
  • IRN (Invoice Reference Number) and QR code generation
  • HS code management and tax calculations
  • Equipment and materials catalog management
  • Buyer and seller registry with NTN verification
  • Compliance reporting and tax liability tracking
  • Multi-company support
