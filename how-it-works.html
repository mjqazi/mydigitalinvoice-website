How It Works

FBR Compliance in Three Simple Steps

Get started in minutes. Our system handles all the complexity of FBR integration so you can focus on your business.

How It Works

01

Create Your Invoice

Add your products with HS codes, enter buyer details with NTN/CNIC, and our system automatically calculates GST/Sales tax based on FBR rates.

02

Auto Submit to FBR

With one click, your invoice is submitted to FBR's IRIS system. Receive your unique IRN (Invoice Reference Number) and QR code instantly.

03

Stay Compliant

Track all submissions, generate compliance reports, and never miss a deadline. Our dashboard shows your complete tax liability at a glance.
