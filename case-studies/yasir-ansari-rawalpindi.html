Customer Story · Trust Rebuilt ↩️ Switched after being burned

Burned by an integrator. Here's why Yasir switched anyway.

A Rawalpindi business owner's story. Paid a previous integrator who took the money and vanished. Why he trusted MyDigitalInvoice — and why he says he'll never work with anyone else again.

Y
Yasir Ansari Business Owner · Rawalpindi, Pakistan

The first time, it went wrong

Before Yasir came to MyDigitalInvoice, he'd already been through the PK SaaS experience most business owners warn each other about. He paid a previous FBR integrator upfront. The integrator took the money and disappeared — the software never got delivered. Months of lost time, lost rupees, and lost compliance-readiness.

This is more common than PK SaaS companies want to admit. Unregulated software market, long integration cycles, upfront fees with no delivery guarantee. By the time business owners realize they've been scammed, FBR penalty deadlines are already overdue.

What changed the second time

When Yasir started looking again, he'd changed his approach. No more paying first. He wanted to meet the people.

"Humara pichla integrator paisay le kar ghaayb ho gaya tha — software develop hi nahi kar saka. Is dafa maine soch liya tha ke pehle logon ko dekhna hai, phir kaam shuru karna hai. MyDigitalInvoice ke owner se baat ki — itne friendly aur responsive ke aisa laga apne family member se baat ho rahi hai. Inhain paison ki koi khaas fikar nahi — yeh product inki passion hai, aur yehi baat system ki ease of use mein saaf nazar aati hai. Poori company ka environment itna acha hai ke ab main aur kisi ke saath kabhi kaam nahi karoonga." — Yasir Ansari, Business Owner, Rawalpindi
In English: Our previous integrator took the money and disappeared — couldn't deliver the software. This time I decided I'd meet the people first. The owner was so friendly and responsive it felt like speaking to a family member. They aren't chasing money — the product is their passion, and that shows in how easy the system is to use. The whole company environment is so good that I'll never work with anyone else again.

Why this matters for anyone who's been burned

Yasir's story is important because trust in PK B2B SaaS is hard-won and easy to lose. If you've been through a bad integrator experience — paid upfront and got nothing, or signed up for software that never worked — your risk tolerance is low. Reasonably.

Three things about MyDigitalInvoice that tend to reassure business owners in that position:

  • Free plan, no card. Start for free. Use it. See if it works. Your first rupee goes out only after you've seen live invoices filed to IRIS.
  • Integration fee paid one week after go-live. Not upfront. We show up and make it work first.
  • Founder-accessible support. Reply "Junaid?" on WhatsApp and you get the founder directly. No distance between the owner and the buyer.

Trust can't be bought with marketing copy. It's built one interaction at a time. Yasir's story is one example of what happens when that works.
