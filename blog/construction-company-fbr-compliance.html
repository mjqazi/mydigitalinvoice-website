How Construction Companies Can Achieve FBR Compliance

February 1, 2026 MDI Team 7 min read

Construction companies in Pakistan face unique challenges when it comes to FBR compliance. From managing multiple projects to tracking equipment and materials, this guide helps construction businesses navigate digital invoicing requirements.

Why Construction Companies Need FBR Compliance

The construction industry is a significant contributor to Pakistan's economy and is under increased scrutiny by FBR. All construction companies registered for sales tax must now issue digital invoices for:

  • Equipment sales and rentals
  • Material supplies
  • Construction services
  • Project milestone billing
  • Subcontractor payments

Common Challenges for Construction Companies

1. Multiple Project Sites

Construction companies often operate across multiple sites. My Digital Invoice's cloud-based system allows invoicing from any location with internet access.

2. Complex HS Code Requirements

Construction materials and services have specific HS codes. Our built-in database helps you find the correct codes for cement, steel, electrical equipment, and more.

Pro Tip

Set up your commonly used construction materials with their HS codes once, and reuse them across all invoices.

3. Progress Billing

Construction projects often involve progress billing based on milestones. My Digital Invoice supports partial invoicing and tracks payments against project totals.
