FBR Penalties for Non-Compliance: What You Need to Know

February 2, 2026 MDI Team 8 min read

With FBR's digital invoicing deadline now in effect, businesses that fail to comply face serious consequences. Understanding these penalties is crucial for protecting your business and avoiding costly mistakes.

Overview of FBR Penalties

FBR has established a comprehensive penalty framework to enforce digital invoicing compliance. These penalties are designed to be severe enough to encourage businesses to take compliance seriously.

PKR 500,000 Fine

For businesses operating without FBR-integrated POS/invoicing software after the mandatory deadline.

Business Closure

FBR has the authority to temporarily seal business premises for repeated non-compliance.

Registration Suspension

Your sales tax registration can be suspended, preventing you from legally conducting business.

Common Compliance Violations

  • Not issuing digital invoices through approved software
  • Invoices missing mandatory elements (IRN, QR code, HS codes)
  • Delayed submission of invoices to FBR
  • Incorrect or missing buyer/seller information
  • Using non-approved invoicing software

How to Avoid Penalties

The best way to avoid FBR penalties is to become compliant as soon as possible. Here's what you need to do:

  • Use FBR-approved software like My Digital Invoice that automatically handles compliance
  • Train your staff on proper invoicing procedures
  • Verify all invoices contain required elements before issuance
  • Keep records of all transactions for FBR audits
